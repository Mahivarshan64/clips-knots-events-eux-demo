Nine Years of
Crafting Moments

From a small setup in Tambaram to Chennai's most trusted event partner — this is how Clips & Knots Events was built.

The Foundation

Built on a single belief: every event deserves precision.

"We don't take on events we can't perfect. That's the only policy we've never changed."

— Mr. Charles, Founder

Clips & Knots Events started in 2015 with a single wedding in Tambaram. Mr. Charles, our founder, had spent years watching events go sideways — not from lack of effort, but from lack of systems. Vendors who didn't show. Decor that didn't match the brief. Catering that ran out before the guests did. He decided to build something different.

In the first year, every event was personally overseen from setup to teardown. That standard never changed as the team grew. Each coordinator at Clips & Knots follows the same protocol: know the brief by memory, arrive before the client, leave after the last guest.

By 2018, word had spread across Tambaram and into central Chennai. Families began referring us to friends not because we were the cheapest option — we never were — but because when they handed us their event, they actually got to enjoy it.

Our catering arm was built in response to client requests. Couples planning weddings kept asking if we could handle the food too. We built an in-house kitchen team with a focus on quality over volume. Our signature biryani became a talking point at every reception we catered.

Today, we manage weddings, corporate events, birthdays, and cultural celebrations across Chennai. We hold a 5.0 rating on WedMeGood, have completed 100+ events, and have never once refunded a client for poor execution — because we don't allow poor execution.

We work with a starting investment of ₹2L+ because quality infrastructure costs money, and we'd rather do fewer events perfectly than many events adequately. That's the Clips & Knots standard — and the reason clients come back.

What Drives Us

Our Principles

01

Precision First

Every item on the brief is treated as non-negotiable. We don't improvise where we've committed. Details that seem small to others are the ones we sweat over.

02

Client Presence

The goal of every event we run is that the client can actually enjoy it. If they're running around managing logistics, we haven't done our job.

03

Honest Pricing

We don't quote low and charge high. Our pricing reflects the work we do. We'd rather lose a booking than take one we can't deliver on our terms.

04

Team Continuity

The coordinator who does your site visit is the same one who stands on your event day. No handoffs. No strangers at your event.

05

Quality Over Volume

We cap the number of events we take per month deliberately. When every event is managed with the same intensity, quality is never a variable.

06

Long Relationships

Many of our clients return for the next milestone — a sibling's wedding, a corporate event, a birthday. We build for trust, not just transactions.
