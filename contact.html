Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should we book?

For weddings, we recommend 3–12 months in advance, especially for peak season (Oct–Feb). Corporate events and smaller celebrations can typically be arranged with 4–8 weeks notice, subject to availability.

What is your minimum event budget?

Our starting investment for full event management is ₹2L+. This ensures we can deliver the quality of service and team deployment that reflects our standards. Catering-only arrangements are quoted separately.

Do you manage events outside Chennai?

Our primary service area is Chennai and surrounding districts. For events in other Tamil Nadu cities, we evaluate on a case-by-case basis. Contact us with your location and we'll let you know promptly.

Can you handle catering without full event management?

Yes. Catering-only bookings are available for events where logistics are already managed. We offer vegetarian and non-vegetarian menus, including our signature biryani, for gatherings of all sizes.

What does the planning process look like?

After initial contact, we schedule a consultation call or site visit. From there: a detailed brief, a concept proposal, vendor coordination, regular updates, and on-site team presence on the event day. One coordinator stays with you throughout.

Do you provide photography or videography?

We work with trusted photography and videography partners and can coordinate this as part of your package. We don't run an in-house studio, but our network includes experienced event photographers we regularly work with.
